About Haribo Gummy Bears

Haribo is a German confectionery company founded in 1920 by Johannes "Hans" Riegel Sr. The company is famous for their gummy bear candy, which was first introduced in 1922 and is still a popular treat today. Haribo gummy bears are known for their unique texture and fruity flavors, which are made from natural ingredients.

Haribo gummy bears are available in a variety of sizes and flavors, including the original flavors of raspberry, orange, strawberry, pineapple, and lemon. In addition to gummy bears, Haribo also produces other gummy candies such as gummy worms, gummy rings, and gummy cola bottles. Haribo gummy candies are enjoyed by people of all ages around the world!
